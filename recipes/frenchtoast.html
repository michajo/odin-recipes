Quick and Easy French Toast

A super quick and easy French Toast for busy mornings!

No need to wait until you have stale bread around.
This fast and easy French Toast recipe will hit the
spot before school or even as a dinner treat!

Ingredients

  • 6 Slices of Bread - You can use whatever kind you have handy, my daughter loves hotdog bun french toast
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 1 Tbsp. Sugar
  • 1 tsp. Cinnamon
  • 2 Tbsp. Butter Plus more for serving
  • Optional - Maple Syrup and Powdered Sugar for serving

Directions

  1. Lightly toast your bread in the toaster, toaster oven, or oven - you aren't looking to make it dark, just dry it out a bit
  2. Beat eggs in a large flat bowl with fork
  3. Add Milk, Sugar, and Cinnamon and mix well
  4. Soak the bread in the egg mixture for about a minute - you want it nice and saturated
  5. Melt butter in a large frying pan or griddle on medium heat
  6. Let excess liquid run off of bread before carefully placing in pan
  7. Cook on each side for about 2 minutes until it starts to brown
  8. Serve immediately with butter, syrup, and a sprinkle of powdered sugar
  9. ENJOY!
