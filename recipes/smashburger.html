Smash Burgers!

There's nothing like a crispy, meaty, cheesy, smash burger!

Ingredients

  • 4 Hamburger Buns - I like the hawaiian style ones
  • 2 Tbsp. Butter
  • 1 Pound 80/20 Ground Beef divided into 8 loose 2 oz. balls
  • Salt and Freshly Ground Pepper - to taste
  • 4 Slices of your Favorite Cheese - American for the classic smash burger
  • Optional Toppings: Lettuce, Sliced Tomato, Sliced Red Onion

Directions

  1. Preheat a heavy, oiled (Preferrably Cast Iron) Pan or Griddle until it just starts smoking
  2. Butter the inside of the buns and grill until nicely toasted
  3. Place the meat balls onto the hot cooking surface with plenty of space in between each
  4. Using a piece of parchment to prevent sticking, smash them down until about 1/4" thick
  5. Sprinkle with salt and freshly ground black pepper
  6. Cook for 30-45 Seconds until the edges are starting to turn dark brown but the center is still pink
  7. Flip and place cheese on half of the pattys
  8. Grill an additional 30 seconds then place the un-cheesed pattys on top of the cheesed ones
  9. Remove them to the buns and serve immediately with your choice of toppings
  10. ENJOY!
